Community Guidelines

By creating a Tiger Talks account and posting on Tiger Talks, you agree to the following guidelines:
  1. Respect the privacy of other users.
  2. Do not share or request private information.
  3. No offensive, disrespectful, or explicit language. This includes harassment, threats, and hate speech.
  4. Keep your content related to its associated tiger space.
  5. Before asking questions, please check to see if they have already been answered.
  6. You must be part of Towson staff/students to post content.
  7. Do not report content without good reason.
  8. No spam. Do not post the same content multiple times.
  9. No cheating or plagiarism. Do not share or ask for any answers to coursework provided by Towson University.
  10. No self-promotion that is irrelevant to the Tiger Space the post is within.
  11. No illegal activity.
