Community Guidelines


By creating a Tiger Talks account and posting on Tiger Talks, you agree to the following guidelines:

  • Respect the privacy of other users.
  • Do not share or request private information.
  • No offensive, disrespectful, or explicit language. This includes harassment, threats, and hate speech.
  • Keep your content related to its associated tiger space.
  • Before asking questions, please check to see if they have already been answered.
  • You must be part of Towson staff/students to post content.
  • Do not report content without good reason.
  • No spam. Do not post the same content multiple times.
  • No cheating or plagiarism. Do not share or ask for any answers to coursework provided by Towson University.
  • No self-promotion that is irrelevant to the Tiger Space the post is within.
  • No illegal activity.
